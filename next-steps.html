Step 5 — What Happens Next?

To ensure transparency and proper handling of the donations, the following process will be observed.

Campaign Payment Confirmation Next Steps
1 Record

Payments are logged for monitoring.

2 Review

The selection committee evaluates recipients.

3 Award

Support is released to selected applicants.

Transparency Process

  1. All donations will be received and monitored by JV Rabang, Batch 2016 Coordinator.
  2. Donation records will be maintained in a publicly accessible Google Sheet. Donor names will be masked for privacy, while relevant payment details will remain available for public review and accountability.
  3. At the close of the donation drive, the total collected amount will be formally remitted to the JPIA Adviser.
  4. The final recipients will be selected by a committee composed of BSA Faculty Members, chaired by Mary Ann Dela Cruz, CPA.
  5. JPIA Adviser Mac Kerwin Lim will facilitate the awarding of donations to the selected recipient or recipients during the first week of August.
  6. Progress updates and relevant announcements will be shared through The Elysians group chat to keep the community informed throughout the process.
