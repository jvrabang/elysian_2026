BSA Batch 2026 CPALE Review Donation Campaign

Step 5 — What Happens Next?

To ensure transparency and proper handling of the donations, the following process will be observed.

Campaign Payment Confirmation Next Steps

Transparency process

  1. JV, the Batch 2016 Coordinator, will receive and monitor the payments.
  2. For transparency, payments will be monitored in a publicly shared Google Sheet. Donor names will be masked, but payment details will be published for public audit and transparency.
  3. JV will remit the payment directly to the JPIA Adviser upon the end of the donation drive.
  4. The selection committee will be composed of BSA Faculty Members, led and headed by Mary Ann Rabanal, CPA. They will select the final recipients.
  5. JPIA Adviser Marc Kerwin Lim will award the donations by the first week of August to the selected recipient or recipients.
  6. Updates will be posted to The Elysians group chat for transparency throughout the process.
