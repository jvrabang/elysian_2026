This campaign aims to raise funds to sponsor one or more graduates from BSA Batch 2026, for their CPALE review. The main coverage is the review fee. Depending on the total donations collected, the campaign may sponsor additional graduates or extend support to other review-related expenses.

Based on publicly available 2026 CPALE review cost guides in the Philippines, review center fees commonly range from around ₱6,000 to ₱16,000+, while broader CPALE preparation costs can be higher depending on format, materials, and inclusions. For this campaign, the practical target is to help cover a review fee of around ₱10,000 to ₱15,000 per sponsored applicant.

Actual sponsorship amount and coverage will depend on total funds raised, selected review center, and the final assessment of the selection committee.

Step 1

Why are we doing this?

The CPA Licensure Examination is a major milestone for every Accountancy graduate. For many students, however, the journey does not end at graduation. Review fees, materials, transportation, food, and other related costs can become heavy financial barriers.

This campaign is our way of helping deserving graduates from BSA Batch 2026 continue their journey toward becoming Certified Public Accountants. Through collective support, even small contributions can help ease the financial burden of review and allow selected graduates to focus on preparing for the board exam.

This is not only a fundraising effort. It is a batch and alumni-led act of solidarity, encouragement, and hope for future CPAs.

Campaign coverage

  • If excess funds are collected and are enough to cover another applicant, the selection committee may extend support to the 5th and 6th applicants
